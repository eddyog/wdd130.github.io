Design Principles

PARC: Alignment

This is a good example of Alignment because it allows us to read and understand easly the main purpose of the website. In this case they are giving a tribute to the Dr. Martin Luther King Jr that is the purpose of the color to to understand more their point Alignment allows an observer to read the image more easily and clearly. This is achieved by aligning objects to establish order and avoid haphazardly positioning items in the image.

PARC: Contrast

We can appreciate the contrast in the image from differenthigns for example the use of the color red. It is a supercar and the red makes to get the attention of the reader to get the perseption of speed Contrast compels the observer to notice important details within the work. Through size, color, or content, contrast attracts the viewer to see two or more objects disagreeing or even conflicting in the image.

PARC: Repetition

The important of Repetition in different ways makes a website to get the attention of the reader in different forms. In this case the purpose is to sell the customer glasses so they are going to use repetition in different styles to get the attention of the client Repetition also gives the image order from repeated use of the same fonts, colors, layouts. This helps to orientate the viewer and also subtly show how certain ideas may be emphasized through reappearances of the same or a similar object.
